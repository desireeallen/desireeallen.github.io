Privacy Statement

We only collect personal information from you in two ways:

  1. We use cookies to analyse your interactions with us. We only use cookies to improve user experience. We record which pages you visit, and the date and length of your visit. Individual records are destroyed within 24 hours once they have been incorporated into our data set.
  2. If you make a booking, your name and contact details will be collected. This information is stored for two years after which it is destroyed.

Providing some information is optional. If you choose not to enter personal information when making a booking, we'll be unable to provide online or phone bookings. You may choose to turn up to the Haukai Restaurant at a date and time of your choosing but we cannot guarantee you a table.

We keep your information safe by storing it in incrypted files and only allowing our Management and selected staff access to it.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you’d like a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at manager@haukai.kiwi.
