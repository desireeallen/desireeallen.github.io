Privacy Statement

We only collect personal information from you in two ways:

  • 1. We use cookies to improve user experience. We record which pages you visit, and the date and length of your visit.
  • 2. If you make a booking, your name and contact details will be collected.

Providing some information is optional.

If you choose not to enter personal information when making a booking, we'll be unable to provide an online booking.

You may choose to turn up to the Haukai Restaurant at a date and time of your choosing but we cannot guarantee you a table.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and only allowing our Management and selected staff access to it.

All personal information is destroyed one year after it is collected.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong.

If you’d like a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at manager@haukai.kiwi.

We will always abide by the Privacy Act 2020.
